Delicious Lemonade Recipe

Picture and Video Credit: allrecipes.com

How to make delicious ice cold lemonade!

Making lemonade only requires a handful of ingredeints. And using the highest quality ingredients will ensure your lemonade turns out to be the best lemonade it can be! With these easy instructions you'll be enjoying an ice cold glass of delicious lemonade in no time!

What you'll need to get started.

  • Large Pitcher
  • Enough Ice to fill the pitcher
  • 5 cups ice water
  • 10 to 20 fresh lemons
  • 2 to 4 cups of white sugar
  • and a few glasses to serve

Recipe Instructions

  1. Squeeze/Juice 9 lemons and set lemon juice aside
  2. Cut 1 lemon into thin slices
  3. Add sugar and lemon juice to pitcher and mix well
  4. Add ice to fill pitcher about 3/4 full
  5. Add half of the lemon slices
  6. Add ice water to fill pitcher
  7. Add ice and 1 lemon slice to each glass
  8. Fill your glasses with lemonade and enjoy!
